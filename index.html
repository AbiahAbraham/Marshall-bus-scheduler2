The Marshall Community Transit Bus Routes. Schedules are subject to change due to staffing.

Follow the rules while riding or you can be suspended from using the community bus.

The Red Route

This route is $1 for a ride and is available everyday of the week.
The Blue Route
This route is $1 for a ride and is availble Monday-Friday.
Dial-a-Ride
This route is available everyday of the week. $2.00 per person when scheudled before 4:30pm. $2.50 per person when scheduled after 4:30pm. $1.00 per each child ages 3-12 years old. free for children 2 years old and younger.
